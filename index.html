Our Story

In the heart of New York City, amidst the bustling streets and vibrant culture, there emerged a culinary gem known as Ecua-Essence. Its story began with Zoila, a spirited Ecuadorian chef with a vision to share the authentic flavors of her homeland with the world. Inspired by her grandmother's cherished recipes and fueled by a passion for cooking, Zoila embarked on a journey to turn her dreams into reality.
With determination and a sprinkle of magic, Zoila transformed a humble space into Ecua-Essence, a haven where Ecuadorian cuisine thrives. Every dish served at Ecua-Essence is a testament to Zoila's love for her heritage, meticulously crafted with the freshest ingredients and infused with the essence of Ecuador. From the savory empanadas that remind her of family gatherings to the aromatic ceviche that evokes memories of coastal adventures, each bite at Ecua-Essence is a celebration of Ecuador's diverse culinary landscape. With warmth and hospitality, Zoila welcomes guests to experience the flavors of Ecuador, inviting them on a journey of discovery and delight.
